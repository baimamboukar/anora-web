How intellibra Works

The Intellibra Kit is a smart wearable device, connected to a software installed on your Smartphone or Tablet.
It is designed to help you monitor your breast health and wellness in the comfort of your home.

Connect The Device

Turn on the physical device and connect it to your smartphone or tablet via Bluetooth in the Intellibra App.

View AI-powered Diagnosis

You view the AI-powered diagnosis right on the Intellibra App and all the data is stored securely on your medical records.

Start Self-checkup

Start your screening by following the instructions on the Intellibra App.

Share with Experts

Share your results with your healthcare provider or Intellibra healthcare experts for further analysis and diagnosis.
